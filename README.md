### 🔬 Research Highlights

#### 🎤 Bangla Speech Emotion Recognition
- **Multimodal fusion** approach combining Wav2Vec2 (audio) + BanglaBERT (text)
- **LODO (Leave-One-Dataset-Out)** evaluation across multiple Bangla datasets
- Exploring 6 fusion strategies including gated fusion and cross-modal attention
